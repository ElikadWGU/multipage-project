Quick information About Sacramento

Sacramento - The Heart of California

Sacramento, the capital of California, is known for its rich history and political significance.

Quick Facts

  • Population: 526,669 as of 2024
  • Incorporated: February 27, 1850
  • Region: Northern California
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income:Median Households Income is $83,753, Which is Lower than the State's Average Income of $96,334

Rich History

Home to the California State Capitol and Old Sacramento, a historic district.

Rivers & Parks

Located at the confluence of the Sacramento and American Rivers, offering great outdoor activities.

Farm-to-Fork Capital

Known for its vibrant food scene and fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

Political & Economic Hub

As the state capital, Sacramento is a center for politics and public administration.
